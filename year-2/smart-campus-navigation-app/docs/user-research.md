# User Research (Summary)

## Target users
- New students
- Visitors and parents
- Students between lectures

## Key needs
- Quick search for buildings/services
- Clear, low-cognitive-load directions
- Accessibility (good contrast, readable text, keyboard support)

## Pain points observed (peer interviews)
- Confusing building names and multiple entrances
- Missing signage or inconsistent naming across maps
- Difficulty estimating walking time
